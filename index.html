PROJECT

Design and Implentation of Soft Starter for 3-Phase Induction Motor
As we know the 3-phase induction motor is self starting.But during starting it draws high amount of current from the supply due to zero back emf,so the motor windings got burned.Hence smooth and slow starting of motor required.In this project the input voltage to the motor is controlled using SCR triggering. SCR is primarily triggered with high delay pulse,then gradually the delay reduces and at the end the delay will be zero.
